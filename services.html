Frequently Asked Questions

Here are the answers to most of the frequently asked questions.

How old do I have to be to participate in a language course?

There is no age limit to participate in a language course. You just need enthusiasm to learn.

Are there any courses for absolute beginners?

Yes, you can enroll in any course as a beginner.

Are certificates given upon a course completion?

Yes, certificates will be provided on completion of the course.

What are the pre-requisites to start learning English?

All you need to begin is dedication, devotion and an interest to learn.

What is the best way to practice English?

Talking by yourself and reading along with the subtitles can aid in improving your English.

What is the average batch size?

Batch sizes will range from 20 to 30 students.
